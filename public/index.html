About Acharya Sushant Sharma

Learn about your trusted astrology consultant

Acharya Sushant Sharma is a renowned astrologer with extensive knowledge in Vedic astrology, numerology, and vastu shastra. With years of dedicated practice, he has helped thousands of individuals find clarity and direction in their lives.

Based in Ghagwal Jatwal, Jammu & Kashmir, Sushant combines traditional Vedic wisdom with modern approaches to provide practical solutions to life's challenges. His accurate predictions and compassionate guidance have earned him the trust and respect of clients across the country.

Specializing in career guidance, relationship counseling, business predictions, and health analysis, Sushant believes that astrology is not just about predicting the future but empowering individuals to make better choices by understanding the cosmic influences in their lives.
